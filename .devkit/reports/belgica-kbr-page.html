Titre
Evangeliarium Xanctense ; Evangéliaire de Xanten ; Evangeliarium van Xanten = [ms. 18723]
Adresse bibliogr.
[Aachen? or Reims?], [9th century]
Collation
221 f. : 2 miniatures ; 26,7 x 22,4 cm
Matériel
Latin manuscript on parchment, containing the canones of Eusebius of Caesarea and the four gospels. One of the main treasures of the Royal Library of Belgium, this manuscript cannot be consulted on grounds of preservation. Very exceptionally, permission may be granted by the curator of the Department of Manuscripts.
Reliure (note)
16th-century binding. In Sankt-Viktor in Xanten from the 10th to the 15th centuries. In 1829 in Maastricht in the collection of J.J. Stiels. Acquired by the Royal Library of Belgium in 1842.
